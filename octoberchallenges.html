October Art Challenges

Every year for October, I revisit a particular unfinished project by participating in art challenges. Below you can see the evolution of the concept, from just one-off character creation towards a larger narrative.

Conceptober 2021

Conceptober was a yearly art challenge spanning the month of October. It was created by @melosprout , @nirryc , and @nuvodraws .
All art seen here was created by me (@mezacreates), with the exception of the background for Day 24 (Greek God) in 2020. Day 24 was a collab between myself and @cramvisualz.
