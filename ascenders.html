The Ascenders

The Ascenders is a 2D puzzle game turned 3D horror. You play as the newest hire at a rock climbing gym. The first section of the game plays as a normal RPG, where you get to explore the setting and characters. As the narrative advances, the player starts to notice some weird occurences in the workplace. This is mirrored by the UI, which starts to be enveloped in pink clouds. At the turning point of the narrative, the UI switches to a 3D first-person POV.
