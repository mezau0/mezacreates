Conceptober 2020

Conceptober is an art challenge spanning October, with different prompts assigned to each day of the month. It was created by @melosprout , @nirryc , and @nuvodraws .
All art seen here was created by me (@meza_creates), with the exception of the background for Day 24 (Greek God). Day 24 was a collab between myself and @cramvisualz.
If you're interested in more of my art, check out my Instagram, Tiktok, or Etsy!
