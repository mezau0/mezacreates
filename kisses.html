Her/She Kisses

COMING SOON!

Her/She Kisses is a dating sim/grammar tool meant to help people familiarize themselves with using unfamiliar pronoun sets in everyday language. Players choose what pronouns they want to practice and who they want to woo. They're presented with a sentence and fill in the missing pronoun conjugations; if they get it right, that's one step forward. If not, that's one step back. Too many steps back will result in the loss of one heart. Hearts determine your date outcome. Lose all 3 lives, and your date runs you over in the Down With CIS bus.
